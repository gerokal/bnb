Quality bed and breakfast located in Barnes, a short distance from Barnes Station, Roehampton University, The National Tennis Centre, The Priory and Barnes Village.


The accomodation offers two lovely bright rooms in a quiet residential road, one a spacious double room at £80 per night and a single at £50 per night.

10% discount for guests staying three nights or more.

Breakfast served in the dining room overlooking a pretty garden. Your hostess has travelled extensively and speaks five languages fluently including Spanish, Italian, French and Portuguese.
